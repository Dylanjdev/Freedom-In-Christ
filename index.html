Our Mission

We seek those who have been rejected and who have tried every other road. Our heart is to help you find the narrow road that leads to God.

We focus on personal relationship with God rather than empty religious tradition.

About Our Pastors

Pastors Chad and Elaine Miles are passionate about spreading the gospel far beyond the walls of the church building.

Street ministry, tent revivals, and door-to-door evangelism are regular parts of our ministry. We go where the lost and hurting can be found and compel them to come and give Christ a try.

We believe in personal relationship with our Heavenly Father through His Son Jesus Christ.

What We Believe

We are a full gospel Bible church committed to sharing the truth of the holy Word of God without a denominational lens.

Our goal is to see people set free, healed, and restored through Christ.
